IDevID

IDevID onboarding enables secure, Zero-Touch onboarding of industrial devices using their built-in manufacturer identity (IEEE 802.1AR). Devices authenticate automatically with their Initial Device ID, allowing Trustpoint to verify authenticity and issue operational certificates without manual intervention.

Zero Touch Onboarding

AOKI (Automated Onboarding Key Infrastructure) enables Zero-Touch onboarding of industrial devices by automatically discovering the owner environment and issuing operational certificates. It builds on device identities and standardized discovery to integrate devices securely without manual configuration.
